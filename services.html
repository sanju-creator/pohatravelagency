Trip to
ujjain

Ujjain, located in the state of Madhya Pradesh, India, is a popular pilgrimage destination for Hindus.The city is home to several famous temples such as Kal Bhairav temple, Mahakaleshwar Jyotirlinga, Mangalnath Temple, and Sandipani Ashram.Mahakaleshwar Jyotirlinga is a Hindu temple dedicated to Shiva and is one of the twelve Jyotirlingas. It is situated on the side of the holy river Shipra and is considered one of the most sacred abodes of Shiva..

Trip to
kedarnath

Kedarnath is a holy Hindu town located in the Rudraprayag district of Uttarakhand, India Kedarnath is one of the 12 Jyotirlingas temples of India and is considered to be very auspicious and most important in the Char Dham Yatra circuit.

Trip to
Adiyogi

The Adiyogi Shiva Statue in Coimbatore is the world's biggest bust statue, carved out of 500 tonnes of steel, standing at a height of 112 feet. It is a part of the popular Isha Yoga Centre and is a symbol of promoting the culture of yoga and meditation. The statue is shiny black in colour and exhibits a moksha theme in yogic culture. Anyone and everyone is invited to the centre to practice the art and better themselves.

Trip to
siddhivinayak

Shree Siddhivinayak Temple, dedicated to Lord Ganesha, is an iconic place of worship in Mumbai. The shrine, which is more than 200 years old, is one of the richest temples in India and frequented by celebrities, Bollywood stars, politicians, and commoners alike. The popular belief is that Siddhivinayak Ganapati will fulfill the wishes of anyone who prays at this temple with a true heart.

Trip to

Trip to
Rameswaram

Rameswaram Temple is a Hindu temple dedicated to the god Shiva located on Rameswaram island in Tamil Nadu, India. It is one of the twelve Jyotirlinga temples and is considered a holy pilgrimage site for Shaivites, Vaishnavites, and Smarthas.

Trip to
Tirupati Balaji temple

The Tirupati Balaji temple, also known as Shree Venkateshwara Swami temple, is a pivotal landmark of Hindu mythology located in the Chittoor district of Andhra Pradesh. It is believed to be the earthly location where Lord Vishnu resides during the Kali Yuga.

Trip to
khatu shyam

The Khatu Shyam Temple is a Hindu temple located in Khatoo village, just 43 km from Sikar City in the Sikar district of Rajasthan. It is a pilgrimage site for worshipping the deity Krishna and Barbarika, who is often venerated as a Kuladevata.

Trip to
